Trekking

Everest Base Camp Trek

Everest is more than a mountain and the journey to its base camp is more than just a trek. Along a route dubbed by some as "the steps to heaven," every bend in the trail provides another photo opportunity — beautiful forests, Sherpa villages, glacial moraines, and foothills. For active adventurers not afraid to break a sweat; it is not a difficult trek.

 

Annapurna Region Trek

Annapurna region is the most diverse & popular trekking area in Nepal. From the full three-week Annapurna Circuit, which stretches into the Mustang region to short 3-day treks .As the name suggests, the centre piece of this part of Nepal is the range of mountains that includes various ranges like Annapurna I, Gangapurna, Nilgiri.

 

Mardi Himal Trek

Mardi Himal is a challenging and beautiful 4-day trek in the Annapurna region that offers breathtaking views of the Himalayan mountain range. It’s a lesser-known route compared to the famous Everest Base Camp trek or Annapurna Circuit trek. The high camp of Mardi Himal is situated at a height of 3850 m. Mardi is a famous route for local and foreign tourists.
